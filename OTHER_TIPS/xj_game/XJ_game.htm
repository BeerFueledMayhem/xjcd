The XJ Game!

Game Objective

You have purchased a used Yamaha XJ motorcycle which has been sitting abandoned in someone's garage for ten years and must revive it into a finely tuned machine before any of your buddies!   This is a game for up to four players.

Instructions

  • Click on the image of the game board to bring up the large version.
  • Print out the large board
  • Cut out the four game peices
  • Beg, borrow, or steal a six sided die

Rules

  • Each player rolls the die to decide who goes first.   Highest roll wins.  In case of tie, the tied players keep rolling until one wins.  Play then proceeds from right to left.   The players choose their respective game peice in this order as well.
  • Each player rolls the die and advances their game peice that many spaces.  If they land on a space that gives instructions, the player must obey the instructions.
  • The first person who arrives at the end wins!
